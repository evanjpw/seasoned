About Seasoned Tech

  • About
  • Description

Welcome to Seasoned Tech, your partner in navigating the complex world of technology leadership and innovation.

Our Mission

At Seasoned Tech, we believe that every company, regardless of its size or industry, deserves access to top-tier technical leadership. Our mission is to provide executive-level tech expertise on demand, helping businesses overcome challenges, seize opportunities, and achieve their technology goals.

Who We Are

Seasoned Tech is the brainchild of Evan Williams, a veteran software engineer and technology leader with over four decades of experience in the industry. We are not just another consulting firm; we are your on-demand CTO, your project rescue team, and your guide through digital transformation.

What We Offer

Our core offering is simple yet powerful: we provide “CTO for hire” services, bringing executive-level tech leadership to companies that need it most. Whether you’re a startup looking to develop your first MVP, an established company embarking on a major software project, or anywhere in between, we have the expertise to guide you to success.

Our services include:

  • Interim CTO Leadership: We step in to provide immediate, high-level technical direction for companies without a current CTO or those in transition.
  • MVP Development: We help startups bring their ideas to life, developing minimum viable products that are scalable, secure, and ready for market.
  • Project Rescue: For troubled projects, we provide the leadership and expertise needed to get things back on track.
  • Digital Transformation: We guide established companies through the process of modernizing their tech stack and processes.
  • Technical Strategy Consulting: We help align your technology initiatives with your business goals, ensuring you’re investing in the right areas.

Our Approach

At Seasoned Tech, we believe in tailored solutions. We begin each engagement by thoroughly understanding your business, your challenges, and your goals. We then bring to bear our vast experience across various technologies and industries to craft a strategy that works for you.

We’re not just advisors; we’re hands-on leaders who can roll up our sleeves and dive into the code when necessary. Our approach combines high-level strategic thinking with practical, on-the-ground implementation.

Scaling to Meet Your Needs

We understand that projects often require more than just leadership. That’s why we’ve partnered with trusted offshore development teams. This allows us to quickly scale up resources when a project demands it, ensuring we can handle projects of any size without compromising on quality.

Knowledge Transfer and Sustainability

We believe in empowering our clients. While we’re here to solve immediate challenges, our ultimate goal is to leave your organization stronger and more capable. We focus on knowledge transfer throughout our engagements, ensuring that your team is equipped to maintain and build upon the work we do together.

Why Choose Seasoned Tech?

  • Decades of Experience: With over 40 years in the industry, we’ve seen it all and solved it all.
  • Versatility: From startups to enterprise-level organizations, we adapt our approach to fit your needs.
  • Full-Stack Expertise: Our experience spans the entire technology stack, from low-level systems to cutting-edge cloud technologies.
  • Business Acumen: We understand that technology should serve business goals, not the other way around.
  • Proven Track Record: Our history of successful projects and transformations speaks for itself.

Let’s Build Something Great Together

Whether you’re facing a specific challenge or looking to take your technology to the next level, Seasoned Tech is here to help. We bring the expertise, the leadership, and the hands-on skills to turn your tech vision into reality.

Ready to elevate your tech strategy? Contact us today for a free consultation. Let’s explore how Seasoned Tech can be the catalyst for your next big technological leap.
