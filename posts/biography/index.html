Meet Evan Williams

  • Biography
  • About

Evan Williams is not just a seasoned technologist; he’s a visionary leader who has been at the forefront of software engineering and tech management for over four decades. His journey is a testament to the evolution of the tech industry itself, from the early days of personal computing to today’s cloud-native, AI-driven landscape.

Early Career and Foundations

Evan’s passion for technology was ignited during his time at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, where he studied physics and computer science. As a founding member of a student-run organization providing facilities for independent research in computer science, Evan demonstrated early on his drive to push the boundaries of what’s possible in tech.

His professional career began in the mid-1980s at ITRAN Corporation, where he cut his teeth on vision tool editing software and microcode debugging. This early exposure to low-level systems programming laid a strong foundation for his future work.

Pioneering in Desktop Publishing and Collaboration

In the late 1980s and early 1990s, Evan played a crucial role in the desktop publishing revolution. At Interleaf, he was instrumental in producing the company’s first WYSIWYG Mac and PC products, reimagining how users interacted with document creation software. His work at Iris Associates on porting Lotus Notes to Mac further solidified his expertise in creating collaborative tools that would shape the future of office productivity.

Entrepreneurial Spirit and Innovation

The mid-1990s saw Evan embrace his entrepreneurial side. As a consultant and later as the founder of CollectM, Inc., he demonstrated his ability to not only architect complex systems but also to lead and inspire teams. His work on digital rights management systems at CollectM was particularly prescient, essentially conceptualizing NFTs long before they became a buzzword.

Leadership in Enterprise Software

As the new millennium dawned, Evan took on increasingly senior roles, including Director of Software Development at CMGion and Director of Product Management at ProvisionSoft. These positions allowed him to combine his technical acumen with strategic thinking, shaping product roadmaps and guiding teams towards ambitious goals.

Mastering Modern Web and Cloud Technologies

Evan’s career has always been characterized by staying ahead of the technology curve. His work at Google and ITA Software showcased his ability to tackle complex problems in airline reservation systems, leveraging modern server and web technologies. At Symantec, he further honed his skills in multi-tier architectures and cross-platform development.

Recent Leadership and Engineering Excellence

In recent years, Evan has taken on executive leadership roles, serving as Head of Engineering at AdeptID and Storeroom Logix. In these positions, he has not only led the development of cutting-edge products but also transformed entire engineering organizations. His focus on introducing structure, improving code quality, and aligning engineering vision with business goals has resulted in successful product launches and improved team performance.

A Polyglot Programmer and Architect

Throughout his career, Evan has mastered a wide array of programming languages and technologies. From C and C++ to Java, Python, Rust, and Kotlin, his polyglot skills allow him to choose the right tool for each job. This versatility, combined with his deep understanding of software architecture, enables him to tackle projects of any scale or complexity.

Commitment to Security and Best Practices

Evan’s experience with SOC 2 Type 2 compliance and his work on security enhancements in various roles underscore his commitment to building not just functional, but secure and reliable systems. His ability to navigate complex regulatory environments while driving innovation is a rare and valuable skill.

Looking to the Future

As Evan embarks on his latest venture as a “CTO for hire” with Seasoned Tech, he brings with him not just a wealth of technical knowledge, but also the wisdom gained from decades of leadership. His unique blend of hands-on coding expertise and high-level strategic thinking positions him perfectly to guide companies through their most challenging tech initiatives.

Whether it’s developing an MVP for a startup, rescuing a troubled project, or helping an established company navigate a digital transformation, Evan Williams is the seasoned expert you want in your corner. His journey through the ever-changing landscape of technology has equipped him with the skills, knowledge, and insight to tackle the challenges of today and anticipate the innovations of tomorrow.
